## Executive Summary & Market Context

Under the current market regime, our multi-factor screening engine has identified **SHRIRAMFIN**, **APOLLOHOSP**, **DRREDDY**, **ADANIENT**, and **TATAMOTORS** as the top 5 high-conviction multi-bagger candidates for the next 3 months.

### Screening Methodology
The **Multi-Bagger Conviction Score (Max 100)** combines the following layers:
1. **Growth Acceleration (25%)**: Revenue and net profit growth rates.
2. **Capital Efficiency & Solvency (15%)**: ROE/ROCE levels and low Debt-to-Equity ratios.
3. **Institutional Smart Money (20%)**: Promoter buying, FII/DII holding changes, and high delivery percentages.
4. **Trend Momentum (20%)**: Support from SuperTrend, ADX trend strength, RSI momentum, and EMA golden crossovers.
5. **Alternative Data (10%)**: App download growth, web traffic growth, search trends, and social media mentions.
6. **Machine Learning Classifier (10%)**: Random Forest probability of a >5% upward breakout within a 30-day rolling window.
7. **Explosive Modifiers**:
   - **Market Cap size bonus**: Small Cap (+15 pts), Mid Cap (+8 pts), Large Cap (0 pts) to reflect compounding velocity.
   - **Beta modifier**: High Beta (>1.35 = +10 pts), Moderate Beta (>1.1 = +5 pts) to factor in volatility sensitivity.

## Risk Management & Position Sizing Guidelines

> [!CAUTION]
> Multi-bagger strategies focus on high-beta and small/mid-cap stocks, which carry elevated drawdown risks if the general market enters a bear or panic regime.

1. **Portfolio Allocation**: Allocate no more than **20% to 25%** of your total capital to high-conviction multi-bagger candidates.
2. **Position Sizing**: Limit individual stock allocations to **5%** for Small Caps and **8%** for Mid Caps to mitigate single-stock tail risk.
3. **Trailing Stop Losses**: Use a trailing stop loss (e.g., ATR-based or 8-12% trailing threshold) to lock in gains as the stock moves upward.
4. **Market Regime Alignment**: If the market regime moves to **BEAR** or **PANIC** (VIX > 22), immediately downsize high-beta exposure and move capital to defensive cash/hedging options.
